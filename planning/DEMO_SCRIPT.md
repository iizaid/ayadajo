# Ayadajo — Demo Scripts

Goal of any demo: make the clinic feel **"this is simple and it solves my real pain"** and end by **creating their trial**. Show, don't lecture. Tie every screen to *their* pain (ask first). Use their language (Arabic) and, ideally, a clinic-shaped sample dataset.

> Golden rule: **show the daily loop, not the feature list.** Less is more. Don't show settings, admin internals, or half-built things.

---

## Pre-demo (always)
- Ask 2–3 questions first so you can tailor: *"كيف بتنظّموا المواعيد حالياً؟ كم no-show بالأسبوع؟ مين بيستخدم النظام أكثر — الاستقبال؟"*
- Have the app open on **Today's Schedule** with realistic sample data, Arabic/RTL.
- Know their pain point and lead with it.

---

## 3-minute demo (quick / busy clinic)
1. **(20s) Hook:** "بوريك بثلاث دقائق كيف بتدير يومك من شاشة وحدة." Mention their pain (no-shows / scheduling chaos).
2. **(60s) Today's Schedule:** show the day at a glance; mark a patient *arrived*; mark another *completed*. "هاد جدول يومك — كل شي قدامك."
3. **(40s) Add patient + book:** search a patient, book an appointment in 3 clicks. "هيك بتحجزي موعد بثواني."
4. **(30s) WhatsApp reminder:** click "تذكير واتساب" → pre-filled Arabic message opens. "بضغطة، التذكير جاهز — أقل no-show."
5. **(20s) Owner number:** flash the dashboard (appointments, no-shows, collected). "وصاحب العيادة بيشوف أرقامه."
6. **(10s) Close:** "بجهّزلك تجربة مجانية ١٤ يوم هلأ؟"

## 5-minute demo (standard)
1. **(30s) Hook + pain.**
2. **(90s) Today's Schedule:** arrived / completed / no-show; the waiting group; search bar.
3. **(60s) Add patient + book appointment** (conflict prevention mentioned lightly).
4. **(60s) Patient profile:** history, last visit, balance — "كل تاريخ المريض بمكان واحد."
5. **(45s) Treatment note + plan (brief):** add a quick note; show a plan with a price/status.
6. **(45s) Payment + receipt:** record a payment; show outstanding; show a printable receipt.
7. **(30s) WhatsApp reminder.**
8. **(30s) Owner dashboard.**
9. **(20s) Trial close.**

## 10-minute demo (interested owner)
1. **(1m) Hook + pain + "built only for dental clinics in Jordan, Arabic, in the browser."**
2. **(2m) Today's Schedule** — full flow incl. waiting room, statuses, quick actions, search.
3. **(1.5m) Patients** — add, dedupe warning, open profile (appointments, notes, plan, balance, files).
4. **(1.5m) Appointment** — book, reschedule, conflict prevention, calendar day/week with doctors.
5. **(1.5m) Clinical** — treatment note in <20s; treatment plan with items/price/status.
6. **(1m) Money** — record payment, outstanding, receipt/invoice.
7. **(1m) Reminder** — one-click WhatsApp; mention email option.
8. **(1m) Owner dashboard** — appointments, new patients, no-show rate, collected, outstanding.
9. **(0.5m) Public booking page** — show `/book/clinic` and the requests queue (briefly).
10. **(1m) Trial + pricing + close** — "١٤ يوم مجاناً، بساعدك بالإعداد، وبنحكي بالسعر بعدين."

## What to show first
**Today's Schedule** — it's the most relatable, most "wow," and most clearly better than paper. Never start with settings or admin.

## What pain point to mention
The one they told you. Default order of resonance: **no-shows → scheduling chaos → lost patient records → no clear numbers.** Frame Ayadajo as the fix.

## How to show each piece
- **Today's Schedule:** "هاي شاشتك الرئيسية" — mark arrived/completed live; show search.
- **Add a patient:** type a name/phone; show instant search + dedupe warning.
- **Book an appointment:** click an empty calendar slot → patient → doctor → service → save; mention "ما بيخليكي تحجزي موعدين بنفس الوقت لنفس الطبيب."
- **Patient profile:** "كل شي عن المريض هون — مواعيده، علاجه، رصيده."
- **Treatment notes:** add a one-line note fast — "الطبيب بيكتب ملاحظة بثواني."
- **Payments:** record a payment; show the balance drop; show a receipt.
- **WhatsApp reminder:** click it; let the pre-filled message appear — very tangible.
- **Owner dashboard:** "هون بتعرف وضع عيادتك بلمحة."

## How to explain the trial
"بفعّلك حساب تجريبي مجاني ١٤ يوم على بيانات عيادتك الحقيقية، وبساعدك بالإعداد. جرّبه على يومك، وإذا ما عجبك ما في أي التزام."

## How to close the conversation
- Assume the next step: "بجهّزلك التجربة هلأ، شو اسم العيادة بالضبط؟"
- Or offer two onboarding times: "نظبّط الإعداد سوا — اليوم بعد الدوام ولا بكرا الصبح؟"
- Always leave with a **scheduled next action** and your WhatsApp.

## What NOT to show in the first demo
- Settings/configuration screens, role/permission internals.
- Super Admin / billing internals.
- Anything unfinished or buggy.
- Advanced/edge features (audit logs, file security details) — mention trust briefly only if asked.
- A long feature tour. **Restraint sells.** If they want depth, do the 10-min version.

## Demo don'ts
- Don't over-explain or read every label.
- Don't promise features that aren't in V1 (be honest about "coming soon").
- Don't get stuck in a bug — have a clean dataset and a backup recording.
- Don't talk price before they see value (unless they ask).
